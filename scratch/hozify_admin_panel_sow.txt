HOZIFY ADMIN PANEL
FUNCTIONAL & DEVELOPMENT SCOPE OF WORK (SOW)
PROJECT OVERVIEW
HOZIFY is a service marketplace and operations management platform where customers can book services, partners can manage businesses and branches, sellers can provide quotations and materials, and administrators can control and monitor the complete ecosystem.
The Admin Panel will act as the central command center for platform operations, user management, partner approvals, booking monitoring, wallet settlements, revenue tracking, reporting, support management, and fraud prevention.
The system must support Role Based Access Control (RBAC), multi-level approvals, real-time tracking, analytics dashboards, and scalable business workflows.
