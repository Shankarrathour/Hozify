HOZIFY ADMIN APPLICATION
Functional Understanding Document
Project Overview
The Hozify Admin Application is a centralized platform used to manage the complete Hozify ecosystem including Users, Service Providers, Business Service Providers (BSP), Business Sellers (BS), Bookings, Quotations, Orders, Payments, Wallets, Referrals, SOS Requests, Services, Materials, GST, Platform Revenue, and Reports.
The Admin Panel will operate on a Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system where each admin will have access only to assigned modules and permissions.
